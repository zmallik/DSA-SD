1. interviewing.io
→ Best for: Tech, PM, data, and engineering roles
→ Anonymous mock interviews with real interviewers from top companies
→ Live feedback on clarity, structure, and depth

2. Pramp
→ Best for: Behavioral, product, and technical interviews
→ Peer-to-peer practice that builds comfort fast
→ Perfect for overcoming nerves through repetition

3. Exponent Interview Prep
→ Best for: PM, TPM, UX, and data interviews
→ Structured questions, mock sessions, and expert feedback
→ Especially strong for role-specific thinking

4. Big Interview
→ Best for: Behavioral and leadership roles
→ Video-based mock interviews with AI-driven feedback
→ Great for pacing, presence, and storytelling

5. Google Interview Warmup
→ Best for: Entry-level and early-career candidates
→ Practice thinking and speaking out loud
→ Quick warm-up before real interviews

6. ChatGPT (used smartly)
→ Best for: Simulated interviews for any role
→ Prompt it to act as a hiring manager and ask layered follow-ups
→ Combine with real feedback for high-impact prep

Pro tip:
Treat every mock like the real thing.
Record your answers
Time your responses
Ask for feedback
